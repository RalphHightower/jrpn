

NOTE:  A "universal APK" can be produced as follows:

    bundletool build-apks --mode=universal --bundle=my_app.aab --output=my_app.apks
    unzip my_app.apks
    distribute universal.apk

Source:  https://testfairy.com/blog/android-app-bundle-how-to-generate-apk-from-aab/
